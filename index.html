About Me

João Sousa was born on 29th September, 2002, in the city of Porto, Portugal. From a young age he has always had the soul of an artist, inherented from his father. In the 3 years of High School, João took the course of Visual Arts. And in 2021, he ventured to the degree in Multimedia in ESMAD. At the time he enrolled in college for Multimedia, the reason for it was that Multimedia is a field that studies the several interests João had, having the most curiosity in learning, at the beggining, about videogame programming and filmmaking. Movies and videogames was always, and always has been, a passion throughout João's life, so this course would be the defining point to decide between one of those career paths. The "issue", was that he started to appreciate equally, or even more other things as well, because of this degree. New passions, like Photography and Animation! João finished his degree on July 2024, and is now in the path of learning more about everything he loves and get better at each one of those things. His endgame dream right now, is to be an Animation Director, write and create new original stories that inspire and entertain audiences around the globe. But meanwhile, as the Jack of All Trades he is, he wants to continue to explore and develop his skill through all other of his passions, whether it's in Animation, Photography, Programming (Web and Videogames) or Illustration.
