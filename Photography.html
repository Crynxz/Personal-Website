Musicians of Bremen

Musicians of Bremen

During my Degree in the subject of Design of Communication, we were challenged to illustrate, in any way we wanted, one of the tales from the iconic Grimm brothers and then make a book out of it, with the illustrations accompanying the text. So, through the art of photography, I decided to illustrate the old tale "Town Musicians of Bremen" through the use of human models disguised as the animals depicted in the story.

In a twisted artistic choice, I home made clay masks of each animal, that then mixed with makeup and ligatures it made for this creepy original look I wanted to go for.
